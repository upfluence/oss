# Colors

OSS provides Upfluence's brand colors, as well as some lighter and darker variations,
along with feedbacks colors. Though there are no specific limitations on where
these colors may be used, it's important to take into account the context and
previous usages when using these colors as part of your design.

## Feedback Colors

Feedback colors are used to indicate the state of an action. In other words, they
give users feedback. Typical feedback states include success, warning, info,
and error/danger.
